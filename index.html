About Me.

I’m Muhammad Azam Raza, a passionate and results-driven web developer specializing in React.js. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I’ve mastered React, from state management and props drilling to building custom components that power dynamic, user-friendly applications. I’ve worked extensively with UI libraries like Material-UI, Ant Design, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS, ensuring sleek, responsive designs. Additionally, I’ve explored Next.js, Nest.js, and worked on various e-commerce platforms like Shopify and WordPress—particularly excelling in Shopify development.

My goal is to help businesses grow by creating high-performance, modern websites that stand out in today’s competitive market. If you’re looking for someone who can turn your ideas into impactful digital experiences, let’s collaborate and make something great!
